Token Information

Token Allocation

  • 50% – Community & Game Rewards:
    A significant portion of tokens will be allocated to players as in-game rewards. This begins with Zombie Invasion and will extend to future titles within the ecosystem.
  • 20% – Team:
    Reserved for the creators and contributors. Tokens may be subject to vesting schedules to ensure long-term commitment and project success.
  • 20% – Listing & Marketing:
    For exchange listings, influencer campaigns, partnerships, and growing visibility and adoption.
  • 10% – Reserve & Ecosystem Growth:
    Held to support future games, community proposals, and ecosystem expansion.

How the Token Gains Value

The token is backed by real in-game economics and external revenue:

  • 50% of revenue from Zombie Invasion will be used to apply liquidity to the token, starting at launch and continuing afterward.
  • This ensures the token has financial backing and growing support as the game succeeds.
  • Future games added to the ecosystem will also allocate a portion of their revenue to token liquidity.
  • Tokens earned in-game will have real utility: trade them, hold them, or use them across titles in our growing ecosystem.
