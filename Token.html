$COAL Token

The official utility token of Zombie Invasion

Overview

$COAL is the in-game reward currency for Zombie Invasion, backed by real player engagement and game sales. This token supports a thriving in-game economy and provides players with incentives tied to performance and achievements.

Tokenomics

  • Allocation:

    • 25% - Development and operations
    • 25% - Exchange listings, fees, and taxes
